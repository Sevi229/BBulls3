About the B.Bulls

The Business Bulls is a collection of sophisticated and exquisitely designed NFTs. Our priority is outstanding quality that captures intricate details of clothing and facial features. Bulls aim to conquer the blockchain and expand the community through their accessible design. To participate in the "play-to-earn" game, own a Business Bull NFT. Use your NFTs to gain access to the Treasury, where monthly payouts reward winners. Exciting developments are planned for the future, including the introduction of new levels and scenery.

Distribution of payouts:

- 1st  place: 50% of the staked Treasury content
- 2nd place: 30% of the staked Treasury content
- 3rd place: 20% of the staked Treasury content

To earn rewards in the web game, connect a Metamask wallet with one NFT and stake it. You get two monthly chances per NFT for high scores.
